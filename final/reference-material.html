Feel the Power of Google Maps

Below you will find the three things that I referenced for this entire project. When using Maps it is essential that you take your time and really understand what is going on. With all of the callbacks it can get very confusing very fast. I recommend adding each piece and checking after each step. In addition your window size will be very important throughout this process. When you are messing with controls you can easily overlap other controls (I did this twice). Another issue I ran into was trying to connect too many features at once. I wanted a drag-able, mark-able, draw-able, cool-able, and code-able map. Well in the process of trying to find that I created so many bugs I had to settle for less, and the real truth is that if you need that many features, go to maps.google.com. If you need something for a quick reference for people, use the API. I hope you learned a lot while reading this!
